Our Rigorous Testing Process

Every refurbished injector undergoes extensive testing to ensure peak performance

1

Idle Test

Simulates the injector's performance at idle speeds to ensure a smooth and consistent spray pattern under low fuel demand.

2

Medium-Speed Test

Tests precision and flow rate at medium engine speeds, ensuring proper fuel delivery during everyday driving conditions.

3

High-Speed Test

Verifies the injector's ability to maintain proper spray patterns and fuel atomization under high engine speeds and heavy load conditions.

4

Leak Test

Checks for any leaks at the injector seals or internal components under pressurized conditions, ensuring complete fuel system integrity.

5

Flow Tests at 44 PSI

Measures the injector's flow rate and spray uniformity at 44 PSI, replicating standard fuel system pressures.

6

OEM-Standard Testing

Each injector is meticulously flow-tested to ensure peak performance with guaranteed flow rate discrepancy of 4% or less across the entire set.

What's Replaced During Refurbishment?

Our fuel injectors go through a rigorous reconditioning process

O-Rings

Replaced & lubricated to ensure a proper seal and prevent fuel leaks

Filter Baskets

Located at the top of the injector, these are replaced to maintain clean fuel flow

Pintle Caps/Nozzle Caps

Replaced if compromised to protect the injector nozzle and ensure proper spray patterns

Seals/Gaskets

All seals and gaskets are replaced & lubricated to maintain a leak-free connection

Spacer Washers

Replaced if applicable to ensure proper alignment of internal parts

Spray Tips

Replaced if damaged to restore proper fuel atomization and spray patterns
